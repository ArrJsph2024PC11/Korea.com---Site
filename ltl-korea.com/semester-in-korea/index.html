    SEMESTER IN KOREA || THE DETAILS

    Our semester in Korea program is unlike any university course you attend because we keep our classes small.

    Our group classes average just three students at LTL.

    The absolute maximum number of students in a class is 10.

    Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention and ample opportunities to practice speaking Korean. By fostering a supportive and interactive learning atmosphere, we create the ideal setting for you to thrive.

    You will study from Monday to Friday, four hours each day, totalling 20 hours a week. Couple this with living with a Korean family, and you’ve got the perfect platform to make huge progress in a very short amount of time.

    We’ve been there and done it countless times – now it’s your turn!

    ACCOMMODATION

    LTL offers a diverse range of accommodation options in Seoul, including homestays and mini-apartments (locally known as goshiwon). Over 80% of LTL students choose to stay with a welcoming homestay family.

    Opting for a homestay in Seoul allows you to immerse yourself in an authentic cultural experience. Interacting with a local family provides a unique opportunity to deepen your understanding of Korean customs and language.

    KOREAN VISA

    As a private language school, we cannot help with the student visa process. You can enroll to our courses without a visa by checking if your country has a visa-waiver agreement with South Korea.

    After you book and finalise the payment for your program, we will issue a Letter of Acceptance and Payment Receipt that you can use to apply for a short-term tourist visa, if needed.
