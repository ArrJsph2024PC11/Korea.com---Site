    LEARN KOREAN IN SEOUL || OUR COURSES

    Full time individual classes are proven to be the best way to reach your language learning goals and pushing yourself towards fluency.

    Coming to learn Korean in Seoul is a hugely exciting and rewarding experience. With LTL you can rest assured you’re getting the most immersive Korean experience there is.

    We offer three different types of Korean language programs. Lessons are 55 minutes long and tailored to your needs, the class schedule can be personalised according to your availability.

    No matter which program you choose, you will become part of the LTL community and be able to attend our weekly social activities with other students.

    Standard

    • 20 hours per week
    • 4 hours of Korean per day
    • Monday to Friday

    Intensive

    • 30 hours per week
    • 6 hours of Korean per day
    • Monday to Friday

    Group & 1-on-1

    • 30 hours per week
    • 4 hours of group class + 2 hours of 1-on-1 class a day
    • Monday to Friday

    ACCOMMODATION

    If you want to make the most of your experience in Seoul, we highly recommend booking a homestay with LTL.

    Approximately 80% of LTL students choose to stay with a Homestay family. 

    You can enjoy life as a member of a Korean family, speak Korean 24/7 and eat delicious homemade food in the comfort of a cosy apartment with one of our fantastic Korean families.

    Living with a homestay has proved to be the fastest way to make progress. Korean families usually don’t speak much English. This creates the perfect immersive atmosphere.

    HOMESTAY IN SEOUL

    KOREAN VISA

    As a private language school, we cannot help with the student visa process. You can enroll to our courses without a visa by checking if your country has a visa-waiver agreement with South Korea.

    After you book and finalise the payment for your program, we will issue a Letter of Acceptance and Payment Receipt that you can use to apply for a short-term tourist visa, if needed.
