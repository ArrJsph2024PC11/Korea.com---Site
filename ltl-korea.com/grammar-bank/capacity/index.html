    HOW TO SAY CAN & CANNOT IN KOREAN

    The most common way to express capacity in Korean can be expressed using 할 수 있다.

    We teach you how to use this structure and more, as well as their respective negative forms.

    CAN IN KOREAN

    Expressing capacity in Korean, and using “can”, “be able to” in a sentence, is done by using 수 있다.

    • When the infinitive of the verb ends with a vowel, the verb is conjugated with ᄅ 수 있다
      • ex: 하다 (to do) → 할 수 있다
    • If it ends with a consonant, the verb is conjugated with 을 수 있다
      • ex: 먹다 (to eat) → 먹을 수 있다
    PATTERN

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수 있다

    CANNOT IN KOREAN

    Negating the sentence is pretty easy, you just have to replace 있다 (there is) with 없다 (there isn’t).

    The sentence structure to express cannot, not able to in Korean becomes:

    PATTERN

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수 없다

    View More Examples:

    TO BE POSSIBLE IN KOREAN

    ~이 가능하다 in Korean is the equivalent of “it is possible” in English.

    • When the noun (often the subject of the sentence) ends with a consonant, the article “이” is used.

    Note the verbs that are normalised in Korean include ~것. This form is treated as a noun that ends with a consonant.

    • When the noun ends with a vowel, the article “가” is used.
    PATTERN

    Noun + 이/가 + 가능하다

    View More Examples:

    TO NOT BE POSSIBLE IN KOREAN

    To negate the previous sentence structure to express “not possible” in Korean, you just have to replace 가능하다 with:

    • Its negative form 가능하지 않다 (it is not possible)
    • Its antonym 불가능하다 (it is impossible)
    PATTERN

    Noun + 이/가 + 가능하지 않다 or 불가능하다

    FAQs

    How to express capacity in Korean?

    Two sentence structures can be used to express capacity in Korean:

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수 있다 is equivalent to “can” in English.

    Noun + 이/가 + 가능하다 is equivalent to “to be possible” in English.

    How to say can in Korean?

    The following sentence structure:

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수 있다 is used to express”can” in English.

    Examples:

    나는 영어를 유창하게 구사할 수 있다.
    I can speak English fluently.

    그는 서두르면 기차를 탈 수 있다.
    He can take the train if he hurries.

    How to say cannot in Korean?

    The following sentence structure:

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수 없다 is used to express”cannot” in English.

    Examples:

    그는다양한악기를연주할수 없다.
    He can’t play many instruments

    그의형은보트를항해할수 없다.
    His brother can’t sail a boat.

    How to express potential in Korean?

    Potential in Korean is expressed by using the following structure:

    Verb + ᄅ/을 수도 있다

    내일 비가 올 수도 있다.
    It may rain tomorrow.
