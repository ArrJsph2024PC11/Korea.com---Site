    ADDITIONAL INFO

    Pre Course Talk

    When you come to learn Korean in Korean, we want to create the perfect group class for you.

    To do this we will organise a pre-course talk before your arrival in Seoul.

    We organise our groups according to levels, so you are matched with a group with the same knowledge of Korean as you.

    Do understand that you are not being tested. This is a casual chat so we can get to know you and your study goals.

    It can be done via WhatsApp, Skype, Zoom or a phone call.

    Other Courses

    We also offer more than just Small Group Courses in Seoul.

    You can also study Korean individually which allows you to choose the content of your course so you can reach your targets even faster.

    We also organise more specific Korean classes such as:

    • TOPIK Exam Preparation
    • Korean for Acting
    • Korean for Auditioning

    If you have any questions, contact us using the form below.

    If you are looking for somewhere to stay we also offer incredible homestay experiences with Korean families. 80% of our students choose to live with a Korean family.

    KOREAN VISA

    As a private language school, we cannot help with the student visa process. You can enroll to our courses without a visa by checking if your country has a visa-waiver agreement with South Korea.

    After you book and finalise the payment for your program, we will issue a Letter of Acceptance and Payment Receipt that you can use to apply for a short-term tourist visa, if needed.
